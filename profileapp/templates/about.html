About Us

We are a team of highly skilled professionals, each bringing unique expertise to the table. Our diverse backgrounds and experiences contribute to a rich tapestry of creativity and innovation. Together, we collaborate seamlessly to address the evolving needs of our community and exceed expectations.

Development Vision

GENSAN... Green City of the South, Financial Center of Region XII, Regional Agri-Industrial and Transshipment Hub of SOCSKSARGEN, and a Model of Harmonious Multi-Cultural City. It aims to have a disciplined, socially-protected, empowered, and educated citizenry, supported by a sustainable and resilient economy.

Organizational Vision

A learning organization delivering total quality service that delights its customers.

Mission

To foster a stable local economy that supports development, environmental protection, and security for all people in General Santos City.

Quality Policy

We, the Local Government of General Santos City, strive to be globally competitive while enhancing the quality of life of our citizens towards a sustainable future.

We are committed to providing quality services to all members of our society.

We shall continually improve our city, our people, and our services while fostering the rule of law and equal opportunity for all.
